Why keep a checklist?

Keeping a birding checklist is an essential tool for birdwatchers of all skill levels. A checklist helps you track the birds you've observed, understand seasonal migrations, and plan future birding outings. Knowing what birds are likely to be in the area you're visiting can enhance your experience and increase your chances of spotting exciting species. Whether you're a casual birder or a seasoned enthusiast, a checklist provides insights into bird behaviors and habitats.

To learn more about birding tips and tools, visit our resources page.
