Our Mission

The Accessible Birding Atlas of Wisconsin aims to make birding an inclusive activity for people of all abilities. This resource highlights birding hotspots with detailed accessibility information, ensuring everyone can experience the joy of birding. By combining interactive maps, user-contributed feedback, and bird species data, the atlas promotes equitable access to nature and encourages conservation through inclusivity.
