Submit a Birding Site

Site Submission Criteria

Please ensure your submission includes the following information and criteria:

  • Parking: Information on available parking spaces and whether they are accessible.
  • Trail: Details about trail surface, length, and conditions.
  • Accessibility: Describe features such as ramps, boardwalks, or any other accessibility considerations.
  • Restrooms: Availability of restrooms, including any accessible facilities.
  • Points of Interest (POIs): Notable features such as bird blinds, observation towers, or scenic spots.
  • GPX File: A GPX file of the trail to help map the site accurately.
