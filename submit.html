Submit a Birding Site

Follow these three steps to add an accessible birding site to the Wisconsin Accessible Birding Atlas.

Step 1: Review Accessibility Considerations

Read Birdability’s Access Considerations to understand what makes a birding site accessible.

Read Guidelines

Step 2: Perform a Birdability Site Review

Review the site and add it to the Birdability Map to contribute to a national effort.

Go to Birdability Map

Step 3: Submit to Our Atlas

Fill out the Wisconsin Accessible Birding Atlas form and upload your site details, including a trail file.

Go to Submission Form
